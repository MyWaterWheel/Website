Everything you need to stay on top of your properties

        

          WaterWheel brings all your key property information into one clean, organized workspace.         

        
          
            

Tenants & Leases

            

              Keep tenant contact details, lease start and end dates, rates, and notes in one place.               See who is in each suite and stay ahead of renewals.             

          
          
            

Maintenance & Tasks

            

              Schedule recurring tasks, log completions, and get alerts when items are overdue.               Attach photos, invoices, or PDFs to each task.             

          
          
            

Utilities & Systems

            

              Track providers, contracts, meters, and major building systems (HVAC, fire, elevators)               with reminders for inspections and warranty expirations.             

          
          
            

Documents & Notes

            

              Store leases, permits, drawings, and key notes so you can find what you need in seconds               instead of digging through email and paper files.             

          
          
            

Vendors & Supplies

            

              Keep vendor contact info handy and track supplies, parts, and reference invoices for               faster repairs and replacements.             

          
          
            

Taxes, Mortgage & Alerts

            

              Optional tools to track property tax and mortgage due dates with helpful reminders               before important payments are due.

Frequently asked questions

        
          
            

Do I have to be a commercial building owner to use WaterWheel?

            

              No. WaterWheel is designed for commercial properties but works just as well for a single home,               cottage, or vacation property. Many users start with one property and expand from there.             

          
          
            

Where can I download the app?

            

              WaterWheel is currently available on Apple’s App Store. Tap the “Download” button above on your               iPhone or iPad, or search for “WaterWheel property management” in the App Store.             

          
          
            

Is my data backed up?

            

              WaterWheel stores data locally on your device. You can use regular device backups and exports               from the app to keep your information safe. Cloud sync options may be added in future updates.             

          
          
            

Can I switch plans later?

            

              Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade between the two-building plan and the unlimited plan through               your App Store subscription settings.
